Recovering Surplus Funds for Homeowners Nationwide

When a property is sold at foreclosure auction for more than the amount owed on the mortgage, the remaining balance is known as surplus or excess funds. Many homeowners are unaware these funds belong to them.

Guardian Recovery Services assists homeowners and heirs nationwide in identifying and recovering foreclosure surplus funds.
