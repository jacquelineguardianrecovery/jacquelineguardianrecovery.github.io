Recovering Surplus Funds for Homeowners Nationwide

When a property is sold at foreclosure auction for more than the amount owed on the mortgage, the remaining balance is known as surplus or excess funds. Many former homeowners are unaware these funds legally belong to them.

Guardian Recovery Services specializes in identifying, securing, and recovering surplus foreclosure funds on behalf of homeowners and heirs nationwide.

Our firm handles research, claim preparation, and coordination with attorneys and courts when necessary. Clients never pay upfront costs. Our fee is a contingency percentage deducted only after funds are successfully recovered.
