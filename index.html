Recovering Surplus Funds for Homeowners Nationwide

When a property is sold at foreclosure auction for more than the total amount owed on the mortgage, the remaining balance is known as surplus or excess funds. Many former homeowners are unaware that these funds legally belong to them. Guardian Recovery Services specializes in identifying, securing, and recovering these funds on behalf of former property owners and their heirs.

Our firm has helped homeowners recover millions of dollars in foreclosure surplus funds. Through careful research, legal coordination, and a nationwide recovery process, we have assisted numerous clients in reclaiming money that would otherwise remain unclaimed. Clients throughout the United States trust our process, and many of our new cases come through referrals from satisfied claimants.

Guardian Recovery Services covers all upfront costs associated with the recovery process, including administrative expenses and legal coordination when necessary. Clients pay absolutely nothing out of pocket. Our fee is structured as a percentage of the confirmed surplus amount and is deducted only after the claim has been successfully approved and the funds are released.

How the Recovery Process Works

1. Surplus Identification

Our research team reviews foreclosure sales and court filings to identify properties where surplus funds exist.

2. Ownership Verification

We confirm the rightful claimant by reviewing property records, probate documents, and ownership history to ensure the correct party receives the funds.

3. Claim Filing & Recovery

Our team prepares and submits the required documentation and works directly with courts and attorneys to secure the release of the funds.
